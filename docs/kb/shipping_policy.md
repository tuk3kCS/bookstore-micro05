## Overview
We support standard delivery to the address provided at checkout.

## Shipment status
- pending: shipment created but not yet handed to carrier
- shipped: handed to carrier, tracking number may be available
- delivered: delivered to customer
- cancelled: shipment cancelled

## Tracking
When a tracking number is available it will be shown in the order detail page.
